Farming Tips & Advice

1. How to Improve Soil Fertility Naturally

Use compost, green manure, and crop rotation to enrich the soil. Adding organic matter improves water retention and nutrient availability.

2. Pest Control Without Harmful Chemicals

Try neem oil spray, companion planting (like marigold near crops), and introducing natural predators like ladybugs to control pests safely.

3. Best Crops to Grow in Summer

Grow heat-tolerant crops like millet, maize, cucumber, okra, and brinjal during hot months for better yield and lower water needs.

4. Water Conservation in Agriculture

Use drip irrigation, mulching, and early morning watering to reduce water usage and improve efficiency on your farm.
